### Undergraduate Courses (本科生课程)

**Medical Physics (医学物理学)**
* **Target Audience**: Freshman (Year 1)
* **Semester**: Fall
* **Description**: This course introduces the fundamental principles of physics as applied to medicine. Topics include biomechanics, fluids, bioelectricity, and an introduction to medical imaging modalities. It aims to build a solid physical foundation for medical students.

**Radiation Physics and Protection (放射物理与辐射防护学)**
* **Target Audience**: Junior (Year 3)
* **Semester**: Spring
* **Description**: Focused on the interaction of ionizing radiation with matter, radiation dosimetry, and safety standards. This course prepares students for clinical environments, emphasizing the principles of radiation protection for both patients and medical staff.

### Graduate Courses (研究生课程)

**Fundamentals of Medical Research (医学科学研究基础)**
* **Target Audience**: Graduate Students
* **Description**: A comprehensive guide to scientific research methodology. The course covers experimental design, data analysis, research ethics, and skills for academic writing and publishing.

**Frontiers in Medical Physics (医学物理前沿)**
* **Target Audience**: Graduate Students
* **Description**: Exploring cutting-edge developments and emerging technologies in medical physics. Topics include nanomedicine, molecular imaging, theranostics, and AI applications in radiology.
