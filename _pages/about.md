**Dr. Qingbin He** is an **Associate Professor** and **Master Supervisor** at the School of Radiology, **Shandong First Medical University**. He received his Ph.D. from the **University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)** in 2021.

In 2024, he was appointed as a **Taishan Scholar Youth Expert** (provincial-level talent). He currently presides over **4 research projects**, including the Shandong Provincial Natural Science Foundation (Youth Project), with total research funding of **650,000 RMB**.

His research interests include:

* **Medical Physics**
* **Nanotechnology**
* **MRI Nano-imaging**
* **Biomaterials & Drug Delivery**

He has published **7 high-level papers** as the first or corresponding author and holds 1 authorized invention patent.
